Telepathic Servos
2026
Wired up two MG90S servos to a Raspberry Pi 4 through a PCA9685 driver, then tied them to a hand tracker running on my laptop webcam. The camera reads my finger positions and moves the servos in real time, kind of like giving the computer a telepathic link to my hand. First real embedded build, and seeing software reach into the physical world felt completely different from shipping another web app.
